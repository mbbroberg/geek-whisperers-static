You have the option to stay and the option to transition out.\u00a0This happened to Scott Abel, CSO of Spiceworks, who we talked to this week on the Geek Whisperers. This interview was recorded live at SpiceWorld 2015 in Austin, TX.<\/p>\n

We spoke to Scott last SpiceWorld<\/a> about the cultural of Spiceworks. This year we hear more of Scott’s personal story.<\/p>\n

Spiceworks was\u00a0the third startup founded or co-founded by\u00a0Scott, and this time they wanted to get it right. By making lists of the\u00a0things\u00a0they wanted to bring to the new company and the things they didn’t want to bring into this new company.\u00a0Some of these lessons came from Scott’s\u00a0time as VP of Professional Services at NeXT, and the lessons he learned (both things To Do and things Not To Do) from Steve Jobs.<\/p>\n
