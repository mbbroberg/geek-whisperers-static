Your Whisperers are back in session and with a brand new guest.\u00a0A self-defined “professional ranter” and online personality in Scott Stratten<\/strong>, aka\u00a0@UnMarketing<\/a>,\u00a0joins us in the pursuit social media done well.<\/p>\n

<\/a>Note:<\/strong>\u00a0Despite our jokes, no one paid anyone to talk on this podcast.<\/p>\n

Scott quickly finds like minds amongst his hosts. We\u00a0hit hard on many topics we’ve explored here on Geek Whisperers:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Using Twitter without a marketing degree<\/li>\n
  • How we all don’t like to hang out with marketing people<\/li>\n
  • That LinkedIn is NOT social media<\/li>\n
  • Going into social media with a purpose<\/li>\n
  • Where is social media headed<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Here are some great quotes from our time together:<\/p>\n

    “If [social media] is a landscape like my front yard, it looks like a bunch of crap.”<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

    “Social media is an untended garden, people planted stuff into it and have no idea why they did it.”<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

    “Social media is this place where everyone is doing a whole bunch of stuff and we don’t know why<\/strong>.”<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

    “You have to have a peer group to build a business. That’s what I went looking for [on social media].”<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

    “Putting social media under marketing only is like putting the telephone under marketing only – it’s a tool: the consumer decides what they want to do with it<\/strong>, not the brand.”<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

    Our time with Scott was spent expanding upon ideas John, Amy and I all believe in. If you also enjoy Scott’s style, we recommend listening into his UnPodcast<\/a>. As always, thanks for listening!<\/p>\n
