Do Tech Recruiters have souls? We’ve often\u00a0wondered. The Geek Whisperers have more faith after hanging out with Andy Tullo. He’s a entrepreneur focused on recruiting in our fast-growing Enterprise Tech scene.\u00a0<\/a><\/p>\n

We discuss:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Vetting company culture before you sign the paperwork<\/li>\n
  • Positive psychology in the workforce<\/li>\n
  • Finding great places to work by matchmaking the right way<\/li>\n
  • Core values of the people in leadership<\/li>\n
  • How\u00a0to\u00a0research as someone looking for a job<\/li>\n
  • Glassdoor as a piece of the puzzle (take it with a grain of salt)<\/li>\n
  • Watch social media of a company<\/li>\n
  • Follow their influencers – do they represent a culture you want to be a part of?<\/li>\n
  • Don’t be scared to reach out to individuals who work for the organization<\/li>\n
  • Asking the right questions<\/li>\n
  • Listen to the\u00a0stories that tell you<\/li>\n
  • Listen for passion<\/li>\n
  • Listen to what was rewarded and what was punished<\/li>\n
  • Do people work for (or quit from) a manager or a company?<\/li>\n
  • Impact of a sloppy hiring practice<\/li>\n
  • Tolerating behavior is a sign of company culture<\/li>\n
  • Careers and lives are intertwined<\/li>\n
  • Seeing\u00a0opportunity in everyone<\/li>\n
  • How do you measure your own success?<\/li>\n
  • Self-awareness<\/li>\n
  • Strength Finder<\/a> as a\u00a0tool toward self-awareness<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Andy is easy to find. Give him a shout on LinkedIn<\/a>, Twitter<\/a>\u00a0or at his company website, Permahub<\/a>.<\/p>\n
